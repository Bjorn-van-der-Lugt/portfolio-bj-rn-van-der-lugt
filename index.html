Joanna Kielar Nails

Joanna Kielar, born and raised in Poland. Currently residing in the Netherlands, she wishes to return to Poland to start her own nail salon. The website I created for her is a simple single-page application that provides a minimal online presence.

Challenges

I was unfamiliar with the beauty industry, which made it difficult to find appropriate images to match the services offered. Additionally, determining the correct image sizes, translating the content into Polish, and staying within the client's minimal budget were challenges I faced.

Toward the end, I also struggled with project-specific burnout due to my perfectionism, which made it challenging to complete the project within my ideal timeframe. I realized, however, that I would always notice things I didn’t like about the project, so I chose to stop where I felt in the moment it was too early to stop, trusting that it was good enough for the client’s needs..

Solutions:

I searched extensively across various stock photo websites and collaborated closely with the client to ensure the chosen images met her needs and were relevant to their respective services.

To handle translation, I had the client verify the Polish text I generated with ChatGPT. This ensured accuracy and allowed the client to provide feedback.

For image sizing, I spent time in discussion with ChatGPT and watched numerous YouTube tutorials about `srcset` and responsive image techniques until I fully understood how to implement them effectively.

The client was very satisfied with the final product and plans to use the site to launch her business. As soon as she has images of her work, I will replace the AI generated images, and stock photo images with images of her work.
